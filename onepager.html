POP · Field Officer Quick Guide

Buying & Grading Plantain
From Outgrowers

Buy good quality · Pay fairly · Record accurately · Handle carefully
1

Acceptable Plantain

Only buy plantain that meets every quality check below.
  • Mature but not overripe — firm, angular fingers
  • Freshly harvested — same day or within 24 hours
  • Clean and free from dirt or latex staining
  • Free from pests and disease damage (no sigatoka, weevil, crown rot)
  • Fingers well formed and of normal shape for the variety
  • Bunch well filled — no missing hands, all fingers present
  • Cut stalk clean — not torn, crushed, or split

4 · Assess

  1. Look at the whole bunch — fill & uniformity
  2. Check finger size and shape against variety
  3. Inspect for defects, pests or disease
  4. Press gently — fingers should be firm, not soft
  5. Check the cut — clean stalk, not torn or split

5 · Weigh

⚖Calibrated scale only. Tare (zero) before each bunch.
📋Record net kg to one decimal (e.g. 25.4 kg).
⚠Shrinkage flag: if net < farm weight by > 5%, flag for review.
📍Weigh at the farm — not at the collection point.

6 · Record

📅Date · Outgrower ID
📍Location / farm
🏷Grade A · B · C · Reject
⚖Quantity (kg) · Price/kg

📸Photo of bunch + signed receipt

7 · Handle & Transport

🌿Handle with care. Avoid throwing.
⛔No heavy loads stacked on plantain.
🚚Clean, ventilated transport vehicle.
☂Protect from sun & rain.
⏱Deliver ASAP to collection point.
Good quality today, better returns tomorrow.
Thank the farmer · encourage good practices · build long-term relationships.
